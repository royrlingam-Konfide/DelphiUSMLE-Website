Meet the Founder

The Mentor Who Changes Trajectories

Dr. Roy Lingam has been training medical students for USMLE Step 1 and Step 2 CK for more than a decade, achieving a remarkable 98% passing rate across his students. His teaching approach goes far beyond passive review — Dr. Roy works directly with each student to build a tailored, individualized study plan that addresses their specific weaknesses and maximizes their strengths.

What sets Dr. Roy apart is his ability to transform overwhelming volumes of basic science material into clear, clinically relevant frameworks that students actually retain. He doesn't just teach you what to know — he teaches you how to think through questions. His students consistently describe breakthroughs in both understanding and exam confidence.

Dr. Lingam is especially known for guiding students who had repeatedly failed the CBSSA and were on the verge of giving up their medical careers — empowering them to turn their setbacks into triumphs. If you've been told the door is closing, Dr. Roy is the person who helps you open it again.
