Research from three leading medical institutions tells us exactly what actually works.

Albert Einstein College of Medicine

"Are questions the answer?" — Students who completed ~2,000 practice questions saw significantly improved results. It's about questions, not just study hours.

2014–2015
University of Michigan

"Study behaviors and USMLE Step 1 Performance" — Students who completed three or more passes through First Aid combined with practice questions were significantly more successful.

Washington University of St. Louis

"Student-directed retrieval" — Practice questions are strongly associated with Step 1 performance. Anki use showed significant correlation. Wellness and mindset matter too.
